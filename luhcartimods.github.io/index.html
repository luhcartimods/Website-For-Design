Hello I am Teun van der Poel, I am a student in Year 10 and was tasked to create a product about a global issue I am passionate about and that I want to spread awarness about, I choose littering as I am passionate about the topic and want to make a change and keep the world clean and make sure the world is clean for the next generation!

In this website you will learn what littering is and its history. We will go in depth about its arrival and when it started to really get bad. We will learn why its such a issue and how it came to be such a issue. We will learn facts about littering, many that will completely change your opinion on the matter. There will also be ways you can contribute and help the cause in as any ways possible to get as many people working against littering, we started the problem so we have to end it!. There is also a activities page which hosts a quiz to test the users knowledge and see if they really did learn anything. The main aim of this website is to teach people about littering in a mroe fun and engaging way, using videos and games to enhance their learning. I want to attract attention to the bad of littering and try teach people how they can combat it in as many ways possible, ultimately we were the ones who started the problem, so we also have to fix it!
