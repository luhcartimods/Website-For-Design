How Do I Contribute Against Littering?

Littering can be combated in many ways, here are some examples:


  • You can promote the cause on Social Medias, The more people you can get to help the better.

  • You can use recycable bags while shopping that you can use every time

  • Pick up litter you see, even if it isnt yours its your planet you live in and want to keep safe

  • Tell your friends and family about the cause and get them to pick up everything

  • Convince local bussinesses to start using more cardboard in their packaging to make sure less plastic circles around
